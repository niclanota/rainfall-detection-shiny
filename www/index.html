Methodology

References

This app demonstrates the use of a not device specific, smart, vision-based rain sensors system. It offers a basic yet useful tool for researchers and citizens to quickly monitor the onset and end of rain-related events, exploiting pre-existing devices with a parsimonious use of economic and computational resources.

Details about methodology and training dataset can be found in

https://doi.org/10.3390/w13050588
